About Us

Planet Care Recycling solves problems within the community by teaching people how to recycle properly and providing recycling programs for students, households, and local communities across South Africa.

Mission

At Planet Care Recycling, our mission is to protect the environment and conserve natural resources by providing innovative and sustainable recycling solutions. We are committed to reducing waste, promoting a culture of recycling, and inspiring our community to work together toward a cleaner and greener future.

Vision

Planet Care Recycling's vision is to enhance environmental awareness and empower local communities through effective, comprehensive recycling practices that create a cleaner and greener future.
